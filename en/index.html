The vision behind EEI/AAI

The domain name of this website, EEIAAI.com, is first and foremost a nod to the Flemish pronunciation of 'AI'. I liked the idea of a domain name made entirely of vowels — not only aesthetically unique, but also fitting, with a healthy dose of humor, for my identity as someone from this region.

It also serves as a backronym for the two fundamental pillars of my work:

EEI: Ethical Expertise for Innovation

Innovation is only true progress when it happens responsibly. I strive for AI solutions that are honest, transparent, and human-centered.

AAI: Accelerating with Artificial Intelligence

Harnessing the power of technology to push boundaries, optimize processes, and solve complex problems faster and smarter.
